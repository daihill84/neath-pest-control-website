Flour Beetle Control

Flour beetles, including the red flour beetle and confused flour beetle, infest flour, grains, and other stored food products. Our control methods focus on identifying and eliminating breeding sites, ensuring your pantry remains pest-free.

Pantry Moth Control

The Indian meal moth is known for infesting a variety of stored food items, including cereals and dried fruits. We offer targeted treatments to eliminate moths and their larvae, protecting your food supply.

Grain Weevil Control

Grain weevils infest whole grains, leading to severe contamination. Our methods focus on detecting infestations early and implementing effective treatments to protect your stored grains from these pests.

Rice Weevil Control

Rice weevils can infest various grains, including rice, and are known for their ability to fly. We provide comprehensive treatments to eliminate rice weevils, ensuring your pantry is safe from these invasive pests.
