My Services

  • Pest Control

    I offer comprehensive pest control services tailored to your needs. Whether it’s ants, spiders, cockroaches, or any other pests, I have the tools and expertise to protect your home or business.

  • Wasp Nest Removal

    Wasp nests can pose a significant threat to your safety, especially during the warmer months. I specialize in safe and effective wasp nest removal, using industry-leading techniques to ensure the nest is safely eliminated while minimizing risks to you, your family, and your pets. Contact me to have a wasp nest assessed and removed promptly.

  • Rodent Control

    Rodents can carry diseases and cause significant damage to your property. My rodent control service includes a thorough inspection, identification of entry points, and the implementation of effective control measures. I provide both short-term and long-term solutions to keep your home or business rodent-free. If you suspect a rat in your attic or a mouse in your house, my methods are safe for children and pets, ensuring a healthier environment for everyone.

  • Insect Control

    My insect control services cover a wide range of pests, including ants, bed bugs, cockroaches, and fleas. I provide targeted treatments designed to eliminate infestations and prevent their return. Using environmentally friendly products, I ensure the safety of your family, pets, and property. Let me help you reclaim your space from invasive insects!

  • Mole Catching

    Protect your gardens and lawns from the damage caused by moles with my expert mole catching services in Neath. I use humane trapping methods to remove moles from your property while ensuring minimal disruption to your environment. I will assess your situation and provide ongoing support to prevent future infestations.
