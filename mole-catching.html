Professional Mole Catching by David

Dealing with mole hills in your garden, farm, or estate? David provides professional traditional mole catching across Neath, Glynneath, Resolven, and Skewen. We offer rapid 24/7 response to ensure your land remains clean and safe.

David's Specialist Mole Solutions:

  • Traditional Trapping: David uses 100% poison-free Duffus and claw traps.
  • Garden Authority: Safeguarding lawns near Victoria Gardens and residential floral borders.
  • Agricultural Safety: Professional clearance for Neath Valley farms to prevent listeria in silage and machinery damage.
  • Heritage Estate Care: Specialist knowledge for historically sensitive grounds near Neath Abbey.

Why Choose David as Your Neath Valley Mole Catcher?

David's mole control prioritizes traditional field skills that are safe for pets, livestock, and children. We avoid toxic gassing and chemicals, focusing on accurate trapping that solves the problem at the source. As an Ex-Pestokil specialist, David brings national standards to the local Resolven community.
