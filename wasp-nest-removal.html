Professional Wasp & Hornet Nest Removal

Facing a wasp infestation? Our specialist wasp nest removal service in Neath and the Neath Valley provides fast, safe, and guaranteed solutions to eliminate nests from your home or business. Wasps and hornets can be highly aggressive; attempting to remove a nest yourself without professional equipment can lead to dangerous stings and allergic reactions.

We serve residential and commercial properties in Glynneath, Resolven, Skewen, Tonna, and Neath town. Our technicians are fully trained to handle infestations in lofts, eaves, sheds, and garden areas.

  • Safe Nest Removal - We use professional-grade insecticides and protective gear to neutralize the nest safely.
  • Guaranteed Results - If the wasps return to the treated nest, we will re-treat it free of charge.
  • Hornet Control - Expert removal of larger hornet nests using specialized techniques.
  • 24/7 Emergency Response - Available round-the-clock for urgent infestations that pose a risk to family or livestock.
