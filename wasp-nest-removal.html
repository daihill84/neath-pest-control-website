Local Expertise: Wasp vs. Hornet Identification

Correct identification is key to safe, effective removal. In Welsh: **Picwnen** (common wasp) vs. **Cacwn** (hornet). Common types we treat:

  • Common Wasp (Picwnen): Smaller, bright yellow & black. Often nests in lofts, wall cavities or sheds.
  • European Hornet (Cacwn): Much larger, reddish-brown & yellow. Highly active at dusk ‚Äî more aggressive when disturbed.
  • Honey Bees: Often confused with wasps. I provide **safe hive relocation** to Neath Valley beekeepers wherever possible (no destruction).
