Expert Rodent Control Services in Neath

Dealing with rats or mice? Welsh Town and Country provides professional rodent control in Neath, Glynneath, Resolven, Skewen, and all surrounding valley areas. Our technicians are available 24/7 to safeguard your home or business.

Rodents pose significant health risks, spreading diseases such as Salmonella and Leptospirosis. Beyond health, they cause structural damage by gnawing through electrical wiring and woodwork. Prompt action is vital to prevent an infestation from growing.

Our 4-Step Rodent Solution

  • 1. Professional Inspection: Identifying entry points and nesting sites.
  • 2. Targeted Treatment: Safe, effective removal using traps or Red Tractor approved rodenticides.
  • 3. Proofing & Exclusion: Sealing holes and gaps to prevent rodents from returning.
  • 4. Expert Advice: Practical tips to keep your property rodent-free long-term.

Specialized Agricultural & Domestic Services

  • Rat Catcher Services: Rapid response for rat activity in gardens, lofts, or farm buildings.
  • Mouse Removal: Humane and efficient techniques to clear mice from kitchens and commercial spaces.
  • Red Tractor Compliance: Specialist pest management for local Neath Valley farms.
