Signs of a Rat Infestation

  • Droppings found near food sources or along pathways.
  • Chewed wires or furniture.
  • Nests made of shredded paper or fabric.
  • Unusual noises coming from walls or attics, especially at night.

If you notice any of these signs, it's crucial to take immediate action to prevent further infestation.
