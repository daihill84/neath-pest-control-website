Signs You Need a Rat Catcher

  • Droppings in kitchen cupboards, lofts, or outbuildings.
  • Gnaw marks on wood, plastic, or electrical wiring.
  • Shredded material used for nesting in quiet areas.
  • Scratching or scuffling noises in walls or under floorboards.
