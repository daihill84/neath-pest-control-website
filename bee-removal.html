Bee Control & Ethical Relocation in Neath

At Welsh Town And Country Pest Services, we prioritize the protection of bees. Our professional bee control services in Neath and Port Talbot are designed to handle infestations while ensuring the safety of these vital pollinators through ethical relocation where possible.

Safe Relocation of Beehives

David (Ex-Pestokil) understands that honeybees and bumblebees are crucial to the ecosystem. We only consider extermination if there is an immediate, unavoidable threat to human safety. Our primary focus is relocation to local beekeepers or safe environments.
